HangmanGame-Alura 🔗

  • The hangman game made in JavaScript is a web application that simulates the traditional hangman game. In this game, a secret word is chosen and the player tries to guess the word by typing letters one by one. For each letter that the player misses, a drawing of a man is drawn, indicating that the player is getting closer to hanging. The objective of the game is to guess the word before the drawing of the man is completed.
