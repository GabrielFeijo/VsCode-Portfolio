Demoday - Kick Off Porto Digital - Digital Boarding

During the months of December and January 2021/2022, students from SENAC, UNICAP and UNIT received a challenge: to create a technology-based solution that contributes to the strengthening of one or more of the 169 goals included in the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (Agenda 2030). And I am happy to share that our team was among the top 10 squads in the Digital Boarding 2021.2 Kick Off.

Presenting the solution to Porto Digital was a great opportunity to showcase our work to other entrepreneurs, mentors, and investors, as well as receive valuable feedback to improve the solution.

For the solution, we thought of creating an app that, in a playful and educational way, assists students and teachers in basic education. The app aims to help teachers teach content in a fun and engaging way, improving learning outcomes for students.
